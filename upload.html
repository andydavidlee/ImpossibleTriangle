UPLOAD you work here!

Step 1
When the upload page is loaded, you wil find the ‘File Upload’ tab already open for you to start. Begin by clicking on the ‘Browse’ button and searching for the file you wish to print.

Step 2
Once you have selected your files click on ‘2. Material Options’ and select which materials you would like your files printed in. You can also select how many copies you want printed.

Step 3
Clicking on the next tab ,‘3. Shipping Address’, you can fill out your Shipping address. Options for saving your address for next time are available.

Step 4
The last tab are options for online payment.

When all 4 tabs are fille out, hit the ‘Submit’ button to process. Any errors will make themself known.
